Short summary:

Story. Heroes of Might and Magic tells the story of Lord Morglin Ironfist, who is forced to flee his homeland through a magical portal, because his cousin, Ragnar, had usurped the throne after his uncle, Ragnar's father, killed Ironfist's father, the legitimate owner of the throne.

Before entering a game:

You need to choose your main town. Towns in this game are 9 in this expansion and they are presented in the tables below. Read through them and choose your favorite. Try not to minmax the game and enjoy it a little since in the begining you will probably choose the lowest difficulty and have a lot of recourses so the cost is not a problem.
